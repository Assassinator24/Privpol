Privacy Policy

AutoReview Social Publisher respects your privacy. This application helps creators and businesses generate and publish original, AI-assisted review content to their own Instagram Business accounts using official Meta (Facebook & Instagram) APIs.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information required to operate the application:

  • Instagram Business Account ID
  • Facebook Page ID
  • Access tokens provided through Meta authentication
  • User-provided content such as captions, scripts, and media
  • Basic usage logs (posting status and timestamps)

We do not collect passwords, private messages, contacts, or personal profile data.

2. How We Use the Information

The collected data is used solely to:

  • Generate AI-assisted review content requested by the user
  • Publish original content to the user’s connected Instagram Business account
  • Maintain posting status and improve service reliability

We do not use user data for advertising or resale.

3. Third-Party Data Sharing

Data may be shared only with trusted third-party services required to provide the app’s functionality, including:

  • Meta Platforms (Facebook and Instagram Graph APIs)
  • AI service providers for content generation
  • Cloud hosting and infrastructure providers

We do not sell, rent, or trade user data.

4. User Rights and Control

Users have full control over their data and may:

  • Revoke app permissions at any time via Meta account settings
  • Request deletion of stored data
  • Stop using the application without penalty

When permissions are revoked, access tokens are automatically invalidated.

5. Content Ownership

This application does not download, store, or repost third-party copyrighted content. All published content is created by or owned by the user.

6. Children’s Privacy

This application is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

This privacy policy may be updated periodically. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
